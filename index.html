All about Cats

Cats are believed to be the only mammals who don't taste sweetness.
Cats are nearsighted, but their peripheral vision
and night vision are much better than that of humans.
Cats are supposed to have 18 toes (five toes on each front paw; four toes on each back paw).
Cats can jump up to six times their length.

Fun Facts about Cats

  • In 1963 a cat went to space.
  • A female cat can become pregnant as young as 4 - 6 months of age.
  • Cats usually sleep around an average of 15 hours per day.
